Overview

IT operations are crucial to the success of any business, but they can be complex and expensive. Konnect IT Workforce provides secure, reliable IT outsourcing (ITO) services that can help you bridge the gap between legacy IT and innovation. We begin by simplifying your existing infrastructure using proven tools and intelligent automation, and we find ways to lower operating costs by up to 30 percent, so you can invest in the future of your IT estate. Konnect is one of the world’s largest providers of ITO and mainframe services, so you can be confident that we will keep your operations running smoothly and securely.

We Support The Following Cooperation Models

Konnect It Workforce provides a wide range of IT solutions designed to meet the needs of any organization, from small businesses to global enterprises. Our experienced team is dedicated to providing cost-effective and reliable services that help you unlock the potential of your company.

Staff Augmentation

If your in-house IT team has any resource or skill gaps, our experts can help fill them. You'll be able to manage them directly, so you can feel confident that your team has the skills and knowledge they need to succeed.

Self-managed Team

When it comes to IT initiatives, a self-managed team led by Konnect's PM or Team Lead is the way to go. This team will take care of everything for you so that you can focus on other things.

Full Outsourcing

We deliver outcome-based solutions tailored to your organization's specific IT needs and requirements. You can focus on your business goals while we take care of the IT function(s) and risks related to them.
